My Project Highlights

Wikipedia Articles

Participation in the “Traductón” event (2024), a collaborative initiative in which students from my university worked together to translate Wikipedia articles. The project consisted of translating content into Spanish, with the aim of contributing to the dissemination of knowledge online and improving the linguistic quality of the articles. In this project, I had the opportunity to translate and work with large terminology databases.

View Project 1 View Project 2

Website Translation

Translation of an informative website entitled Terminology and GAI, focusing on the relationship between terminology science and generative artificial intelligence. The work consisted of translating the content from English into Spanish, maintaining the conceptual clarity of specialized topics such as the semantic triangle, knowledge graphs, and communication between humans and machines.

View Project

Localization Management Core Competencies

Translation and localization of an interactive website for self-assessment of localization skills. The project consisted of translating the content, maintaining the structure and functionality of the website, which allows users to reflect on their professional skills and assess their level in different localization-related competencies.

View Project

The Nature Conservancy Translation

Translation and localization of The Nature Conservancy website, an interactive project developed that presents educational content about Burmese pythons (Python Patrol). This project consisted of translating the interface and texts from English to Spanish, ensuring that the instructions, explanations, and interactive elements were clear and understandable for Spanish speakers in Miami.
